A POOL COMPANY LIKE NO OTHER

TRAINING

Kirby Aquatics offers exceptional lifeguard certification classes, placing our graduates in the top 1% of lifeguards in the nation. KA lifeguards meet or exceed the most stringent certification requirements in the nation. Lifeguarding is serious, so we take it seriously. What's more, we offer discounted certification and scholarships for candidates who couldn't otherwise afford classes.

SERVICE

When Kirby Aquatics services your pool, you never have to worry... you never even have to think about it. Your pool is taken care of the right way, on time, every time. Because our focus is on safety and serving others, you can feel good knowing that a portion of your pool service costs are helping those less fortunate by subsidizing services in under-served communities... and our rates are still competitive!

CONSULTING

Kirby Aquatics partners with commercial pool operations to provide objective assessment of physical facilities, processes and procedures, and staff readiness. A professional evaluation from KA can save you money on insurance premiums, but more importantly, make your swimmers safer. We offer sliding scales and multiple service tiers to keep our services attainable for a wide range of communities.

LESSONS

Nothing keeps swimmers safer than improving their swimming skills. At Kirby Aquatics, we believe everyone should have the opportunity to learn to swim. We offer a wide range of swimming lessons, from private, in-home lessons, all the way to discounted (sometimes free) group lessons for under-served populations. Our instructors work with all ages and abilities to make better, safer swimmers.
