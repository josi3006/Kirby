All About Kirby

Kirby Aquatics exists for two simple, but radical, reasons...

  • We love pools! We love working at pools, in pools, and on pools. Kirby Aquatics is a company where pool people can work and have fun doing what they love!

  • We love helping others! Whether it's providing great service, assisting commercial operations, or teaching people, we know the heart of service... is serving.


We think these reasons make us different, and better.

All of our employees enjoy coming to work and doing what they do. We think that employees who love their work do a better job. So we only hire people who love pools, and we only do the work that they love.

That's why attitude is the most important hiring qualification we have. Skills can be taught, but attitude is natural. All of our employees come to work because they love it.

That's also why we don't burn ourselves out by trying to do everything. We don't build pools because we aren't construction people; we don't sell patio furniture because we're not... well... furniture salespeople. We just love taking care of pools and the people who swim in them. If that's a radical idea, we're OK with that.

Here are some practical ways that our radical ideas make us different (and better):

  • All of our employees hold official certifications for what they do. Most have years of experience.

  • Part of our revenues go to serve those who wouldn't otherwise enjoy our sevices, in the form of class scholarships, sliding-scale fees, and community outreach.

  • Kirby only provides three services: pool service, consultation, and training.

  • Our communication is thorough, electronic and simple; we think it's most efficient and effective that way.
