CONSULTING

Whether you're planning a new facility, or just need a new look at an older facility, Kirby Aquatics can galvanize your team around a standard of safety and efficiency. Our holistic approach looks at every aspect of your operation, engages your team at every level, and gives you a roadmap to success.

What does that success look like, really? It means your facility will be safer for both patrons and staff, and your operation will save money. Kirby Aquatics can give your staff the tools, training and mindset to create a safer place; we can upgrade your physical facilities, often for little or no cost, to cut down on accidents and increase readiness; and we can help revise your policies and procedures to streamline your operations.

What this all means is that your staff and patrons are happier; you have fewer accidents; and ultimately, your costs go down.


Consulting Options


Kirby Aquatics takes a holistic approach by looking at three core areas:

Staff Effectiveness

Your operaton is only as good as your least-performing employee. Knowing this, Kirby Aquatics seeks to improve your staff at all levels, regardless of position. Your staff will be observed, tested, trained, coached and retested to achieve the best performance possible. In addition, Kirby Aquatics will help you optimize your recruiting, training, management and compensation processes to achieve the best staffing results for your operation; and we will assist in creating performance metrics and measurement tools to maintain consistent excellence.

Physical Integrity

The most visible and, arguably, the most impactful aspect of your operation is the physical facilities. Kirby Aquatics will evaluate your buildings, grounds, equipment, supplies and environment to identify problems and suggest solutions; in addition, Kirby Aquatics will target practical improvements at any and all cost points to help you improve your facilities.

Operational Efficiency

Kirby Aquatics will evaluate your administrative and managerial procedures and materials, then help you to establish sound practices for a strategically efficient operation. You'll be able to work less, and achieve better results by streamlining, or even eliminating, burdensom processes. The goal is to free your staff from "busy work", to concentrate on operating an excellent facility.

So how does it work? We have several consulting options available:

  • All-Season 360 Partnership
  • Our most effective program is the A360 Partnership. Kirby Aquatics partners with you throughout a full season, providing regular visits to get your operation into top shape. We begin the season with an announced visit to get a baseline assessment of all three core areas. From this visit we create a guidebook with customized, practical steps for improvement. Later, Kirby Aquatics will return throughout the season, with both announced and unannounced visits, to assess your progress. The season ends with a debriefing to include a season wrap-up, and a look forward to the next season.

  • All-Season Core Partnership
  • Like the 360 Partnership, the Core Partnership lasts a full season, but concentrates on just one core aspect of your operation. Perhaps you're having staffing issues and want to fix them. Maybe your administration is in shambles. Kirby Aquatics will focus our efforts on your specific issue by listening to you, understanding your needs, then providing concrete steps to move you forward. Kirby Aquatics will make several visits throughout the season to assess and tweak your improvements, to get you where you want to be, and keep you there.

  • Step-Up 360 Program and Step-Up Core Program
  • Our Step-Up Programs tackle either your entire operation, or one particular Core area. We call these programs "Step-Up" because they encompass only two visits, rather than the whole season. The visits are intended to help you step up your game from where you are, to where you want to be, in just a short time. In both Step-Up Programs, Kirby Aquatics will make an initial assessment visit to generate a baseline understanding of your issues, then provide customized guidelines with concrete steps for improvement. On our second visit, Kirby Aquatics will verify and document the steps you've taken and advise you on how to keep up the improvement.

  • Open Consultation
  • Our Open Consultation option gives you control over the scope, duration and content of our relationship. At an hourly rate, Kirby Aquatics can provide whatever help you need. Here are some examples of work you might find useful:

    - Evaluating and revising architectural plans for a new facility to optimize lifeguard coverage.

    - Offering objective advice on filtration and sanitization upgrades.

    - Conducting one-time staff training on a particular topic.

    - Creating administrative materials that are streamlined, simple and electronic.

    - End of season shut down of your facility.

    - Writing a new employee manual.

  • Every facility is unique, so there are an infinite number of unique problems. Whatever you have going on, let us know. Our Open Consultation option lets you contract with us for any kind of help we can provide.

I'd like to learn more; what should I do next?

We would love to meet with you for an initial introduction and to learn more about your situation. There is no charge to meet, and there will be no pressure to commit to a contract. We just want to learn more about your operation and to answer any questions you might have.
