POOL SERVICE

The Kirby Aquatics pool service team has one goal: to make sure you never have to think about us. When you partner with Kirby Aquatics for pool service, our professionals will show up on time, every time, and do what's right to make sure your pool is clean and safe. You'll never wonder why we didn't show up, or why your pool is cloudy. You'll never wonder about anything, because we take care of it, on time.

Kirby Aquatics guarantees* your service schedule, so you'll always know when we're coming. We keep the lines of communication open, and the information flowing, so you'll always know what's going on with your pool. What this all means is that you won't have to think about Kirby Aquatics once you're on board. We take care of your pool the right way, on time.


Service Options


Kirby Aquatics offers three tiers of service to satisfy your wants:

All-Inclusive

Our All-Inclusive pool service puts your mind truly at ease. When you book our top-level service option, we'll take care of your pool's chemistry and cleanliness all season long; in addition, we will de-winterize and open your pool in the spring, shut down and winterize your pool in the fall, and take care of incidental repairs and supplies*, for one fee. You won't have to worry about paying for various services, repairs, etc. It's all included!

Deluxe

Our mid-tier service level includes the basics for getting your pool up and running in the spring; keeping it clean, safe and ready all summer long; then winterizing it in the fall. Our Deluxe service includes opening and closing your pool for the season, as well as keeping your pool chemistry balanced and the pool clean all season long, all for one fee. This tier does not include incidental supplies and repairs, which will be charged separately as needed and approved.

Basic

Our Basic service includes regular maintenance to keep your pool clean and balanced. This is our most affordable service, for those who prefer to handle many of the pool maintenance tasks themselves. Our team will visit your pool on a regular schedule to check the chemicals, apply what's needed, and to keep your pool clean. What's not included is opening and closing the pool for the season,or any repairs and incidental supplies.

* The fine print:

  • On-Time Guarantee
  • Kirby Aquatics guarantees that we will show up on your scheduled service day, or we will provide that day's service for free as soon as posible. Of course, there are exceptions! This guarantee doesn't count when we can't provide service because of circumstances we can't control, like severe weather, locked gates, etc. When this happens, we will still provide service as soon as possible, usually within 24 hours.

  • All-Inclusive Service
  • In addition to opening and closing your pool, balancing the pool chemistry, and keeping your pool clean and healthy, the Kirby Aquatics All-Inclusive Service plan also includes incidental repairs and supplies. What this means is that Kirby Aquatics will provide whatever chemicals are needed to keep your pool chemistry in balance, even if we need to use special/non-typical/non-regular chemicals. What this also means is that Kirby Aquatics will provide small/incidental repairs for free...no parts charges, no labor charges...free. It's like an insurance policy against those little annoyances that can nickel-and-dime you all season long.

  • In general, what's included are repairs to things you can see and touch without getting in the pool. What's not included are repairs to things you can't see and touch, repairs to things that require getting in the pool, replacement of major parts and equipment, and things that aren't part of the pool. Some examples of things that are covered are things like:

    - visible pipe leaks
    - pump motor repair
    - skimmer weirs
    - pool cover anchor repairs

    What's not included are things like:

    - underground pipe leaks
    - underwater repairs
    - pump motor replacement
    - new pool covers
    - pool furniture
